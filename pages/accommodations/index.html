Accommodations

Our wedding (both ceremony and reception) will be taking place at the Oceanview of Nahant in Nahant, MA. If you look in the top left corner of the map widget below, you can find the street address of our venue; if you click the "directions" link next to the street address, a new tab will open in your browser with Google Maps open, with the Oceanview of Nahant as the end destination. You need only put in the starting location, and Google Maps will give you instructions to our venue.

It is a bit of a drive from the highway, but once you cross over the land bridge into Nahant, you're almost there!

No need to worry about parking, the venue has plenty of spots.

Nahant is a small beach town, and as a result there aren't any hotels there. However, there are lots of options for hotels in the surrounding cities. A few are listed below for your convenience:
